Common Questions

What if I have more than 2 dogs?

Basic and Standard plans include up to 2 dogs. Additional dogs are just $5/mo each. The Premium plan covers unlimited dogs.

Do I need to be home?

Nope! Just make sure we have gate access and we'll handle everything. We'll text you when we're done.

What areas do you cover?

We serve the entire Pomona HOA community in Manvel, TX and surrounding neighborhoods.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Pause or cancel your plan whenever you need to.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes! Refer a Pomona neighbor and you both get $10 off your next month. Just have them mention your name when they sign up.
