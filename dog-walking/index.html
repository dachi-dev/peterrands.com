Common Questions

How long is each walk?

Each walk is a full 30 minutes of active walking time, not counting leash-up and return. Need 60-minute walks? Just use two punches per visit.

Do walks expire?

Punches are good for 6 months from the date of purchase, so you can use them at your own pace.

What if I have more than one dog?

One walk covers up to 2 dogs from the same household at no extra cost. 3+ dogs add $5 per walk.

How do I schedule walks?

After purchase you'll get a link to book individual walks by day and time. Reschedule or cancel up to 4 hours before with no penalty.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes! Refer a neighbor and you both get a free walk. Just have them mention your name when they sign up.
