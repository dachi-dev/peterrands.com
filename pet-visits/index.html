Common Questions

Who are we?

Pet Errands is a locally owned, family-run business based in the Houston area. Every Pet Pawvisor we send is background-checked, interviewed in person, and has extensive hands-on experience with pets. Learn more about us.

What happens during a visit?

Each visit is 15 minutes. We'll let dogs out for a quick potty break, feed cats, refill food and water, scoop litter boxes, and give your pets a little love and a wellness check. Just tell us what your pets need when you sign up.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Pause or cancel your plan whenever you need to.

What if I have more than two pets?

One visit covers up to 2 pets from the same household at no extra cost. 3+ pets add $3 per visit.

What's the difference between a Pet Visit and a Dog Walk?

Pet Visits are 15-minute in-home check-ins — perfect for potty breaks, feeding, water refills, and quick care tasks. Dog Walks are 30 minutes of active walking for exercise and energy burn. Pet Visits work for any pet; Dog Walks are dog-specific.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes! Refer a neighbor and you both get a free visit credit. Just have them mention your name when they sign up.
