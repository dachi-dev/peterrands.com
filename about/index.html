Why We Started Pet Errands

The idea for Pet Errands came out of our own struggle. Between work, kids, and long Houston commutes, we were constantly scrambling to keep up with the small but important things our dogs needed — midday potty breaks, vet runs, grooming appointments, and the never-ending yard cleanup. We tried the big apps, the random sitters, and the "I'll do it myself after work" approach. None of it felt right.

We wanted someone we could actually trust with a house key and a family member. Someone reliable, someone local, someone who treated our dogs the way we do. When we couldn't find that, we decided to build it — for our neighbors and for ourselves.

Pet Errands started with a simple promise: show up on time, treat every pet like our own, and make life a little easier for the families we serve. That's still the whole point.
